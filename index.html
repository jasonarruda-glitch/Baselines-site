Our Story

Performance is physical. It is mental too.

Baselines was created around a simple belief: the right gear should do more than look good. It should help athletes feel ready, stay focused, and compete with confidence.

That is why we started with a product athletes use every day and built a message into it they can carry onto the court, field, track, or training floor.

This brand begins with socks, but it stands for something bigger: preparation, resilience, and the ability to respond when the moment gets hard.
